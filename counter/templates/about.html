About Nutri Track

My journey with food and health has been a rollercoaster . I used to struggle with understanding nutrition, often eating without knowing what fueled my body.

That's when I realized knowledge is powerespecially when it comes to what we eat. Nutri Track was born from my passion to make nutrition tracking simple, fun, and accessible for everyone!

Why I Created This

One of the biggest gaps I noticed in existing calorie tracking apps is how they fail to cater to people with specific health conditions. Many apps focus solely on calorie counting and nutrient tracking, but they don't take into account the unique needs of individuals managing health issues like diabetes, heart conditions, or other chronic illnesses.

Nutri Track was designed to bridge this gap. It isn't just a tool to count calories, it's a tool to help users track and understand their nutritional needs based on their health conditions, making it easier to manage their wellness goals in a meaningful way.

Whether you're dealing with a specific health condition or simply aiming for a healthier lifestyle, Nutri Track is here to support you on your journey
