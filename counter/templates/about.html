My Nutri Track Journey

My journey with food and health has been a rollercoaster. I used to struggle with understanding what I was eating, often consuming meals without realizing their nutritional value. Over time, I realized that knowledge is power—especially when it comes to nutrition.

That’s why I created Nutri Track. It's not just an app; it's a reflection of my personal experience with tracking calories, making informed food choices, and learning about what fuels my body.
